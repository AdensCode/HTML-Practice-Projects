Bob's Halal Beef Burger Recipe

Juicy Halal burger with simple spice mix and classic toppings.

Here is the recipe

You wanna start off on the meat

Preparing & Pre-seasoning. A good beef burger often is determined by the spice mix into the beef patty:

ingredients

  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp cayenne pepper (optional for heat)

Instructions

  1. Mix the spices into the ground beef gently to avoid overworking the meat.
  2. Divide the mixture into 4 equal portions and shape them into patties.
  3. Preheat your grill or skillet to medium-high heat.
  4. Make a slight indentation in the center of each patty.
  5. Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side.
  6. Add cheese in the last minute of cooking if you like.
  7. Toast the burger buns until lightly browned.
  8. Assemble with lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, and sauces.
